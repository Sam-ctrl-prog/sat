Fast Track Plan

Plan Summary

  • Duration: 2 weeks
  • Intensity: High
  • Mock Tests: 1-2 per week
  • Daily Study: 3-4 hours

Who is this for?

The Fast Track Plan is perfect for students who have limited time before their SAT exam. This intensive two-week plan is designed for those who need to maximize their preparation in minimal time, focusing on high-impact strategies and targeted practice. Ideal for students who have some familiarity with the SAT format but need a comprehensive review and strategy boost before test day.

Plan Highlights

  • Daily intensive study sessions (3-4 hours)
  • 1-2 full-length mock tests per week
  • Strategic content review focused on high-frequency topics
  • Daily practice sets targeting all three test sections
  • Emphasis on error analysis and mistake prevention
  • Test-day strategies and time management techniques
